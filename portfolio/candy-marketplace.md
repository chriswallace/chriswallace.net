  <!-- Introduction -->
  <section class="case-study-section">
    <p class="fade-in-element">When I joined Candy Digital's marketplace for digital collectibles, the platform was facing a crucial inflection point. While the core technology was solid, users were struggling to understand the true value of digital collectibles, and the platform's design language varied significantly between primary drops and secondary listings.</p>
    
    <p class="fade-in-element">The marketplace needed to evolve to better serve both newcomers and experienced collectors alike. As lead product designer, I embarked on a comprehensive redesign that would transform how users discover, understand, and collect digital items.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- The Problem -->
  <section class="case-study-section">
    <div class="case-study-callout case-study-callout--problem fade-in-element">
      <h3>The Core Problem</h3>
      <p>Users couldn't understand what made digital collectibles valuable. The platform's design language was inconsistent, making it hard for newcomers to learn while frustrating experienced collectors. The challenge wasn't just about aesthetics—it was about crafting an experience that could effectively communicate the unique value of each collectible while making the platform more intuitive and engaging.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Homepage Redesign -->
  <section class="case-study-section">
    <h2 class="fade-in-element mb-6">Reimagining the Homepage as an Educational Journey</h2>
    
    <p class="fade-in-element">I began by reimagining the homepage as an educational journey, introducing users to the world of digital collectibles through compelling visuals and clear messaging. The goal was to help newcomers understand what makes digital collectibles special while giving experienced collectors quick access to what they need.</p>

I paid special attention to highlighting the tangible benefits of ownership, from physical redemptions to exclusive content access.</p>
