  <!-- Introduction -->
  <section class="case-study-section">
    <p class="fade-in-element">Immigration processes are notoriously complex, overwhelming, and anxiety-inducing. When Plymouth Street approached me with their MVP web application, they had a solid foundation—but users were struggling to submit the correct documents, understand their application status, and move forward confidently.</p>
    
    <p class="fade-in-element">Over an intensive six-week period, I was tasked with completely overhauling their product experience. The challenge wasn't just about aesthetics—it was about transforming a confusing, high-stakes process into something approachable and empowering.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- The Problem -->
  <section class="case-study-section">
    <div class="case-study-callout case-study-callout--problem fade-in-element">
      <h3>The Core Problem</h3>
      <p>Plymouth Street needed to serve two distinct user groups with very different needs: anxious visa applicants managing life-changing paperwork, and busy HR professionals coordinating multiple applications. The existing MVP couldn't handle the complexity of immigration requirements while remaining approachable to users in high-stress situations.</p>
    </div>

    <p class="fade-in-element">I started by auditing the existing MVP and interviewing team members to understand the biggest pain points. What I discovered was a system that asked too much of users at once—complex legal information presented without clear hierarchy, unclear next steps, and no sense of progress through an inherently overwhelming process.</p>

  </section>

  <section class="case-study-section">

    <p class="fade-in-element">My first priority was developing a dashboard that would serve as the foundation for all applicants. The goal: help users see exactly where they are in the process and what they need to do next—nothing more, nothing less.</p>

    <p class="fade-in-element">One of the biggest sources of anxiety for visa applicants is document management. Missing or incorrect documents can delay applications for months. I focused on creating workflows that guide users through document uploads with clear instructions and real-time validation.</p>

Each component was designed with interactive states, accessibility considerations, and detailed documentation for the development team.</p>
